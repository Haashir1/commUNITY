What is commUNITY?

 CommUNITY is a vibrant and compassionate platform dedicated to uniting individuals from diverse backgrounds and empowering them to create lasting change. In collaboration with the MCWS (Muslim Community of Western Suburbs) masjid, our mission extends beyond religion and culture as we strive to make a difference in the lives of individuals and communities.

 At the core of CommUNITY is our belief in the power of collaboration. Together with MCWS masjid, we address social issues, promote inclusivity, and support those in need. Through local donation drives, we ensure that your contributions reach those who genuinely need them, particularly in Michigan, home to a large refugee population.

 Volunteering is crucial to our mission. You can actively contribute by helping package donations or assisting in their transportation. We offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your availability, and volunteering with CommUNITY counts towards fulfilling CAS or NHS hours.

 We take great care to ensure that donations reach deserving individuals. Working closely with MCWS masjid, we verify the eligibility of recipients, prioritizing those most in need. To further enhance transparency and fairness, we are developing an application system reviewed by a special council appointed by MCWS. This additional step will strengthen our efforts in providing targeted support.

 Refugees face numerous challenges when starting anew in a different country. In Michigan, we extend support to refugees by providing resources, guidance, and assistance with housing, healthcare, and education. Our partnership with MCWS masjid allows us to ease their transition and empower them towards a brighter future.

 CommUNITY is a vibrant platform that fosters connections and creates positive impacts. Together with MCWS masjid and our community, we can build a stronger, more connected society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.
