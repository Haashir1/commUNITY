Welcome to commUNITY

Have a desire to give locally, but could never figure out how? In need of a quick and secure way to obtain free items? Perhaps interested in volunteering? Well, this is the place for you!!

Interested in Donating?

Looking to make a local donation? Rest assured, our streamlined procedures ensure your items reach those who genuinely need them. Michigan, home to one of the largest refugee populations in the US, offers a profound opportunity for your contributions to positively impact countless lives in our community.

Interested in volunteering?

Want to make a meaningful impact in your community? Join us at our drop-off centers and contribute by helping package donations. If you have a vehicle, you can also assist us in transporting donations over the weekends. Whichever option you choose, rest assured that flexible scheduling options are available. Moreover, your efforts will count towards CAS or NHS hours.

What is commUNITY?

CommUNITY is a vibrant and compassionate platform dedicated to fostering connections and making a positive impact in communities. In collaboration with MCWS (Muslim Community of Western Suburbs) masjid, our mission is to bring people together, uniting individuals from diverse backgrounds and empowering them to create lasting change. With a strong belief in the power of collaboration, our motto "Joining Hands, Changing Lives" encapsulates our commitment to collective action and the transformative influence it can have on society. Together with MCWS masjid, we strive to address critical social issues, promote inclusivity, and support those in need. From organizing local donation drives to facilitating volunteer opportunities, commUNITY provides a space where individuals can actively contribute to making a difference. Join us and MCWS masjid as we work hand in hand, creating meaningful and lasting change in the lives of individuals and communities alike.
